Protecting Kids in the Digital World...

Stay Safe Online 🌐

Learn cybersecurity habits and protect children from online dangers.

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity helps protect devices, games, and personal information from hackers, scams, and cyberbullying. Every child should learn safe internet behavior.

Smart Safety Rules

🔐 Keep passwords secret
👨‍👩‍👧 Ask parents before downloads
🚫 Don't chat with strangers
⚠ Report anything scary

Online Dangers

  • Cyberbullying
  • Fake Games & Links
  • Online Scams
  • Identity Theft

Guide for Parents

Monitor online activity, enable parental controls, and communicate openly with children about internet safety.
